Different opportunities for you to serve the Lord

Worship team

Join our passionate worship team, where you can use your musical talents to lead our congregation in heartfelt worship experiences that draw us closer to God

Media Team

Use your creativity and technical skills to serve on our media team, helping to spread the message of faith through engaging content, graphics, and digital outreach efforts.

Protocol team

Join our protocol team, where you can assist in facilitating smooth and orderly church services and events, helping to ensure that all attendees feel welcomed, valued, and cared for.

Security team

Serve on our dedicated security team, ensuring the safety and well-being of our congregation during gatherings and events, providing peace of mind and a secure environment for worship and fellowship.

Kingdom Financiers

Partner with us as Kingdom Financiers, contributing your financial resources to support the various ministries and initiatives of our church, enabling us to fulfill our mission and impact our community for Christ

Kingdom Business Center

Discover how to integrate your faith into your business practices by engaging with our Kingdom Business Center, where we encourage ethical business principles and entrepreneurship that align with Kingdom values.
